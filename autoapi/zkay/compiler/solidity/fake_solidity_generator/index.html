zkay.compiler.solidity.fake_solidity_generator.replace_with_surrogate(code: str, search_pattern: Pattern, replacement_fstr: str = '{}')¶

Replace all occurrences of search_pattern with capture group <keep> (if any) + replacement.

Replacement is either

  1. replacement_fstr (if replacement_fstr does not contain ‘{}’)

  2. replacement_fstr with {} replaced by whitespace corresponding to content of capture group <repl> (such that replacement length == <repl> length with line breaks preserved)

The <repl> capture group must be the last thing that is matched in search pattern
